Portfolio
A portfolio is a collection of work designed to showcase one's skills and accomplishments,
mainly for the purpose of proving capabilities to potential employers or clients, mainly in the creative field.
It should be well-organized, highlighting high-quality pieces that reflect personal branding. Regular updating and tailoring for specific opportunities will enhance its effectiveness in communicating one's unique value.
PROJECT BIDDING
A project bid is a proposal from a contractor or service provider stating the scope of work, time,
price by which they would complete a project. Clients will review several bids using criteria such as price, experience,
approach in order to find the best fit for a provider.
FREELANCING
A freelancer is a self-employed professional who works and gets paid on a per-project basis for different clients.
They work independently, setting their own schedules and handling business tasks such as invoicing and customer outreach.
